THE STORY

I'm a video editor and content creator with a passion for transforming raw footage into compelling visual stories. Every cut, every transition, every color grade is a deliberate choice.

My work spans brand films, social media content, music videos, and documentaries. I believe in the power of pacing — knowing when to hold a shot and when to cut creates emotion that words alone never could.

Whether you need a 60-second reel that stops the scroll or a full-length documentary, I bring the same obsessive attention to craft to every frame.
